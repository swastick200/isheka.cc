Regret that will never ended

Khud se kiye wade, ab toote ja rahe hain,
Bure dor ke andheron mein khud ko fir khona chaha raha hoon.
Haath mein jalti cigarette, jam ka woh khaali glass,
Nashon ki karwahat mein teri hothon ki mithaas.

Tujhe dard diye, aur khud ko bhi ek saza di,
Teri hansi ke badle sirf tanhai aur ashkon ki nami di.
Tu sirf solah ki thi, ek masoom roshni,
Aur main, ek toofan, jo tera har sapna le gaya kabhi.

Tere aansuon ke har bojh ko tune chupke jhela,
Main to bas ek gham tha jo tere sapnon pe tha phela.
Har ek zakhm tera, mujhe jeene nahi deta,
Har yaad teri, mere armaanon ko rakh kar jalata.

Aaj bhi ek wada nibha raha hoon, chah kar bhi,
Tere alawa kisi aur ka ban na saka, har baar wahi.
Tere aansuon ka dard, har raat rulata hai mujhe,
Aur teri yaadon ka saaya mujhe baar-baar dikhata hai.

Tere ahsaan ka bojh, kaleja jala kar utaar raha hoon,
Par khud se, khud hi ko har baar maat de raha hoon.
Bas ek ahsaan aur kar, mujhe khud se azaad kar,
Ya phir mujhe apna kar le, meri har saans tera haq kar.

Agar nahi toh bas ek nazar pyar se dekh le mujhe,
Mujhe khud ke hone ka ek pal ka ehsaas de,
Aur phir, chahe maut bhi aa jaye, koi shikwa na hoga,
Tere pyaar ke ek pal ke liye, duniya chhodna bhi manzoor hoga.

Agar phir se janam mile, toh is baar tere khwab ke saanjhe mein rahunga,
Teri chaahat ka waisa hi insaan ban kar tere saath chalunga.
Har galti sudhar kar, sirf mohabbat hi nibhaunga,
Aur is duniya mein bas tere saath apna jahan basaunga.

"Every regret is a reminder that my heart still beats only for you."
